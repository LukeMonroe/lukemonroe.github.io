Iced Caramel Latte

Overview

Yield: 24 oz iced coffee

Cook Time: 5 minutes per 24 oz iced coffee

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup concentrated medium roast coffee
  • 3/4 cup oatmilk
  • 1 tbsp torani caramel syrup
  • 2 cups ice

Utensils

  • liquid measuring cup
  • cocktail shaker

How To Make

Step 01

Brew the coffee. The coffee needs to be concentrated for this recipe to taste right.

Step 02

Put the ice in the cocktail shaker. Pour the coffee over the ice, then the caramel syrup. Close the cocktail shaker and shake for 5 seconds.

Step 03

Open the cocktail shaker and pour the coffee into the container you'll be drinking the coffee out of. Make sure there is still room left in the container for the oatmilk. Add the oatmilk and give the drink a light stir.

Step 04

Fill any remaining room in the container with ice and enjoy!
