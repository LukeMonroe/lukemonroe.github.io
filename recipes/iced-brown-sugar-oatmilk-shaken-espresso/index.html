Iced Brown Sugar Oatmilk Shaken Espresso

Overview

Yield: 1 coffee

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup medium roast coffee
  • 1/2 cup oatmilk
  • 2 tbsp light brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups ice

Utensils

  • liquid measuring cup
  • cocktail shaker

How To Make

Step 01

Brew the coffee. Use the specialty brew option if your coffee maker has it. Since this recipe is using regular coffee instead of espresso, the coffee needs to be more concentrated. If your coffee maker doesn't have a specialty brew option, add twice the amount of coffee grounds you would normally use to brew a cup of coffee.

Step 02

Combine the light brown sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla extract in the liquid measuring cup. Then add 3 tbsp of cold water to the mixture. Give it a quick stir and place it in the microwave for 35 seconds. Be sure to watch the mixture in the microwave so it doesn't boil over the edge.

Step 03

Place roughly 1 cup of ice in the cocktail shaker. Pour the light brown sugar mixture over the ice, then the coffee. Close the cocktail shaker and shake for about 15 seconds.

Step 04

Open the cocktail shaker and pour the contents into the container you'll be drinking the coffee out of. Make sure there's still some room left in the container for the oatmilk. Now add the oatmilk and give the drink a light stir.

Step 05

Add the remaining 1/2 cup of ice to the container and enjoy!
