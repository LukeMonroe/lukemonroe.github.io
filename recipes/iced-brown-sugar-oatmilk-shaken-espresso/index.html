Iced Brown Sugar Oatmilk Shaken Espresso

Overview

Yield: 24 oz iced coffee

Cook Time: 5 minutes per 24 oz iced coffee

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup concentrated medium roast coffee
  • 2/3 cup oatmilk
  • 3 tbsp cold water
  • 3 tbsp light brown sugar
  • 1/3 tsp cinnamon
  • 2/3 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups ice

Utensils

  • liquid measuring cup
  • cocktail shaker

How To Make

Step 01

Brew the coffee. The coffee needs to be concentrated for this recipe to taste right.

Step 02

Mix the light brown sugar, cinnamon, vanilla extract, and cold water together in the liquid measuring cup. Give the syrup a quick stir and place it in the microwave for 40 seconds. The syrup may try to boil over the edge, stop the microwave before that happens.

Step 03

Put the ice in the cocktail shaker. Pour the coffee over the ice, then the syrup. Close the cocktail shaker and shake for 10 seconds.

Step 04

Open the cocktail shaker and pour the coffee into the container you'll be drinking the coffee out of. Make sure there is still room left in the container for the oatmilk. Add the oatmilk and give the drink a light stir.

Step 05

Fill any remaining room in the container with ice and enjoy!
