Italian Meatballs

Overview

Yield: 30 meatballs

Cook Time: 15 minutes per baking sheet

Ingredients

  • 32 oz ground beef
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 2 tsp basil flakes
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt

Utensils

  • large bowl
  • baking sheet
  • ice cream scooper

How To Make

Step 01

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees. Leaving out the ground beef, mix all the ingredients together in the large bowl.

Step 02

Place the ground beef in the large bowl and fold it onto itself until everything is evenly mixed.

Step 03

Line the baking sheet with tin foil. Using the ice cream scooper, fill the baking sheet with golf-ball sized meatballs.

Step 04

Place the meatballs in the oven for 7 1/2 minutes. Take the meatballs out of the oven and flip them all over. Place the meatballs in the oven for another 7 1/2 minutes.

Step 05

Take the meatballs out of the oven and separate them from the grease on the baking sheet.

Step 06

Repeat the last 3 steps for each baking sheet.

Step 07

Let the meatballs cool off for 5 minutes and enjoy!
