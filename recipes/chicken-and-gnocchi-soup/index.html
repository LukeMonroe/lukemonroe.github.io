Chicken And Gnocchi Soup

Overview

Yield: 1 soup

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 16 oz cubed chicken breast (cooked)
  • 16 oz mini potato gnocchi
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups half-and-half cream
  • 2 cups baby spinach leaves
  • 1 cup celery
  • 1 cup onion
  • 1/2 cup carrots
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Utensils

  • 1 large pot with lid
  • 1 cutting board
  • 1 wooden spoon

How To Make

Step 01

Cut up the celery, onion, and carrots into small bite-sized pieces. Combine the olive oil, celery, onion, carrots, garlic, pepper, and salt into the large pot.

Step 02

Place the large pot on the stove and set the burner to a medium low heat. Stirring occasionally, let the ingredients simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 03

Stir in the cubed chicken breast and chicken broth. Stirring occasionally, bring the mixture to a light boil.

Step 04

Stir in the mini potato gnocchi and baby spinach leaves. Let the ingredients cook for 5 minutes.

Step 05

Stir in the half-and-half cream. Let the ingredients cook for another 5 minutes.

Step 06

Turn off the burner and place the lid on the large pot. Let the soup marinate for 10 minutes and enjoy!
